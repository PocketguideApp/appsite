Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PocketGuide ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in relation to our mobile application and website.

1. Data Sovereignty & Collection

PocketGuide is founded on the principle of data sovereignty. We do not collect, process, or store any personal data, identifiers, or sensitive information. Our application operates without a centralized backend, ensuring your information never leaves your device.

2. Local Storage & Database

All application state, including collection progress, favorites, and user settings, is stored exclusively in a local SQLite database on your device. This data is not synchronized with any external cloud services by the application.

3. Algorithm Engagement

The "Guide Assistant" feature utilizes an on-device retrieval algorithm to process user queries against a static local dataset. No telemetry or conversational data is transmitted to third-party AI providers or external servers.

4. Third-Party Integration

We do not utilize third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, or tracking. We do not sell or share information with any third parties, as no such information is collected.
